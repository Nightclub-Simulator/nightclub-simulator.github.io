FAQ & Information

What is the Infection mode?

The Infection mode is a new gamemode where you have to infect everyone before the time runs out, or survive as the last human standing!

How do I access the beta version?

Follow the tutorial steps on this site to switch to the beta release channel in your Meta Quest app library.

Is this mode available for all players?

Yes! As long as you own the base game, you can access the beta test. While Infection is multiplayer, you can test the new mode without having the multiplayer DLC!

What should I do if I encounter bugs?

Please report any bugs or issues you find in our Discord server.

When will Infection be released officially?

The official release date will be announced once testing is complete and all major issues are resolved.

Can I play with friends?

Yes! Infection supports multiplayer matches where you can compete against your friends.
