FAQ & Information

What is the Battle Royale mode?

The Battle Royale mode is a new competitive gameplay feature where multiple players compete for the first place!.

How do I access the beta version?

Follow the tutorial steps on this site to switch to the beta release channel in your Meta Quest app library.

Is this mode available for all players?

Yes! As long as you own the base game, you can access the beta test.

What should I do if I encounter bugs?

Please report any bugs or issues you find in our Discord server.

When will Battle Royale be released officially?

The official release date will be announced once testing is complete and all major issues are resolved.

Can I play with friends?

Yes! Battle Royale supports multiplayer matches where you can compete against your friends.
